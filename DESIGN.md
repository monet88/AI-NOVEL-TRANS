# AI Novel Weaver - Design System (Impeccable)

## Design Philosophy
The application follows a premium SaaS aesthetic ("Impeccable") tailored for an immersive, distraction-free reading and editing experience suitable for long-form fiction translation.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: `Outfit` (sans-serif) for all UI elements and content, providing a modern, round, and highly premium SaaS feel.
- **Visual Rhythm**: Focus on generous line heights and tracking to avoid eye strain during long translation sessions.

## Color Palette (Tinted Dark Mode)
- **Theme**: Dark Indigo
- **Backgrounds**: Deep indigo gradients with solid bases (e.g., `#0B0F19` for main background, `#161C2C` for sidebars/panels).
- **Text**: Warm white (`#F3F4F6` or `#F9FAFB`) for primary text to reduce harsh contrast; soft muted indigo for secondary text.
- **Accents**: Subtle indigo/purple highlights for active states, avoiding overly saturated primary colors.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Translation Workspace**: Dual-column parallel layout (Left EN, Right VI).
- **Borders**: Removed harsh borders; replaced with generous padding and background color differentiation.
- **Active Row Focus**: The currently edited row is subtly highlighted with a soft indigo tint and a visual indicator on the left edge.

## Motion & Micro-interactions
- **Fluidity**: Soft slide-ins for sidebars.
- **Fade-ins**: Staggered fade-ins for glossary and chapter lists.
- **Feedback**: Smooth hover and focus states for all interactive elements. Skeleton loaders with shimmer effects.
