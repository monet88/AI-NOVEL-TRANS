# AI Novel Weaver - Product Strategy

## What
AI Novel Weaver is a browser-based translation workbench for long-form fiction. It pairs human editors with AI providers (Gemini, OpenAI, DeepSeek, Local MT) to produce genre-aware Vietnamese translations of English novels — primarily wuxia, xianxia, and modern urban fiction.

## Target Audience
- Solo translators working on web novel serializations.
- Translation groups managing multi-chapter projects with consistent terminology.
- Hobbyist readers who want quick draft translations of untranslated novels.

## Key Workflows
1. **Genre-aware translation:** Built-in Sino-Vietnamese transliteration rules.
2. **Glossary-first extraction:** Extract, review, and enforce terminology before translating, ensuring consistency across hundreds of chapters.
3. **Batch processing:** Translate or extract glossary for multiple chapters in one operation with progress tracking.
4. **Side-by-side editing:** Translation workspace uses a split-pane layout to view source and target text simultaneously, facilitating accurate translation.
